Create your first estimate on BestMate app

1. Click 'Add Estimate' from the '+' sign in the bottom bar.

2. Add the client's name from the list or create a new client.

3. Add a product or service by clicking 'Add Product' or 'Add Service,' which will also be stored for future use in the Product/Service list.

4. Alter prices as needed.

5. Save or send your first estimate.
